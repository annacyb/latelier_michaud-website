Responsibility

In Atelier Michaud, we strongly believe in contributing to a better and greener future of the jewellery industry, and all our activity and interests, from producing to selling and after-caring, is carried out with a strong focus on UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) 3 “Good Health and Well-Being” and 13 “Climate Action”.

Choices

We strongly believe in our own responsibility towards climate change and supporting the STG 13, “Climate Action” by constantly searching for and implementing sustainable solutions whenever possible. All our jewelry is made using only bio gold, recycled sterling silver and certified precious stones, and our jewelry boxes, bags and displays are made from recycled/upcycled materials.
