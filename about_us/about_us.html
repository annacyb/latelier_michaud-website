Atelier Michaud

Founded in Copenhagen, Atelier Michaud is crafted and nurtured by its owner, Pauline Michaud. All the jewellery is handcrafted with recycled high quality silver and gold. The brand’s aim is to create unique design and support Danish industry.

The journey of the brand started in the very early childhood of Pauline as she has always been seeking new ways for creative self-expression. From making jewellery from old beads, she has grown both personally and professionally and moved to Paris to attend business school.

After the French adventure, she returned to Denmark to start education in “Jewellery, Technology and Business” at KEA in 2018. This is when Atelier Michaud has come to life – Pauline had a chance to pursue her dream as a jewellery designer and started designing and producing the pieces in her own apartment.

Pauline is drawn to organic and delicate shapes - mix of sophistication and modern bohemian. She gets inspiration from the wild nature and little nuances in day to day life. Currently, she is looking at outsourcing the production to Thailand to a small workshop to improve the technique and provide better product for her customers.
