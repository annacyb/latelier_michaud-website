Our values

Our brand

Founded in Copenhagen, Atelier Michaud is crafted and nurtured by its owner, Pauline Michaud. All the jewellery is handcrafted with recycled high-quality silver and gold.

The brand’s aim is to create unique designs, drawn by organic and delicate shapes - a mix of sophistication and modern bohemian.
